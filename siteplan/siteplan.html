Overview

Purpose

This website supports the Ball Forward project, which helps young people and adults to practice sports such as soccer, beach volleyball, footvolley and other physical activities. It shows which of these projects are closest to you. It introduces young people to professional soccer clubs, helping young people to have a professional career as a soccer player. It promotes events in which other people become interested in the project.

Scenarios
